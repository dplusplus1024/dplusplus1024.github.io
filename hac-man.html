Bitcoin Script Challenge

Did you know that bitcoin has its own, native smart contract language? Bitcoin Script is a Forth-like, stack-based langauge, where operations will either consume or manipulate elements on the stack.

In this challenge, you task is to evaluate a bitcoin script. The remaining stack elements will serve as your password to advance to the next Hac-Man level.

Bonus: funds are locked in this UTXO by the script.
